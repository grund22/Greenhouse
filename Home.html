HISTORY OF MISS BONNIE'S

Bonnie Travis was the eptimoy of a pioneer for forging her way as one of the first women entrepenuers in the small town of Mt. Washington Kentucky. Bonnie started Travis Daycare in 1967 and was the first to start a preschool program with a certified teacher on her staff. Also, being the first daycare in the area. Over the daycare had expanded for her house to bigger spaces, and acquiring another location. Bonnie then decided to open a flower shop, then a balloon shop. She was responsible for establishing 4 businesses, a entrepenuer way ahead of her time.
